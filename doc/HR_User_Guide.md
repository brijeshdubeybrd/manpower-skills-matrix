# HR & Manpower Matrix - User Flow Guide

## 1. Accessing the Portal
**Purpose**: Secure entry for HR and Department Heads.

*   **Step 1**: Open the application URL.
*   **Step 2**: **Email Entry**
    *   Enter your corporate email address (e.g., `user@raymond.in`).
    *   Click **"Sign In"**.
*   **Step 3**: **OTP Verification**
    *   You will receive a One-Time Password (OTP) on your email.
    *   *(For Demo purposes, use OTP: `123456`)*.
    *   Enter the OTP and click **"Verify & Enter"**.

---

## 2. Dashboard Overview (Home Screen)
**Purpose**: Central hub for viewing manpower data and skills competencies.

Upon logging in, you are directed to the main **Dashboard**.
*   **Header**: Displays "Manpower & Skills Matrix", your User Profile, and a **Logout** button.
*   **Filter Bar**: Located at the top. Use this to drill down into specific data:
    *   **Function**: (e.g., Marketing, Sales)
    *   **Band**: (e.g., Band 1A, Band 3)
    *   **SBU**: (Strategic Business Unit)
    *   **Role**: Specific Job Role.
    *   *Note: Filters are interconnected (selecting a Function updates available Roles).*

---

## 3. Viewing the Competency Matrix (Default View)
**Purpose**: Visual representation of skills across different bands.

*   **Default View**: The system opens in **"Skills Matrix View"** by default.
*   **Layout**:
    *   **Rows**: Data is organized hierarchically by **Band** (Band 1A to Band 5).
    *   **Columns**: Divided into "Functional Skills" and "Leadership Skills".
*   **View Modes** (Top toggle buttons):
    *   **Functional Skills**: Highlights only technical/functional competencies.
    *   **Leadership Skills**: Highlights behavioral and leadership competencies.
    *   **Clear View**: Shows all data.
*   **Interactivity**:
    *   **View Details**: Click on any specific **Skill Name** to open a detail panel on the right.
    *   **Proficiency**: Visual "dots" (1-5) indicate the required proficiency level for each skill.

---

## 4. Managing Employee Records (Admin & Edit Mode)
**Purpose**: For HR to view raw data, update proficiency levels, or remove records.

*   **Step 1: Switch to Table View**
    *   In the top-right header, click the **Table Icon** button (next to the Grid icon).
    *   The view changes from the visual Matrix to a detailed **Data Grid**.

*   **Step 2: Enable Admin Mode**
    *   Click the **"Switch to Admin View"** button above the table.
    *   The button will turn purple and say "Admin Mode On".

*   **Step 3: Editing Data**
    *   **Update Proficiency**: Click on the colored proficiency badge (e.g., "3") in any row. A dropdown will appear; select the new level and press Enter or click away to save.
    *   **Delete Record**: Click the **Red Trash Icon** at the end of a row to permanently remove a record.

*   **Reset Data**: A "Reset Data" button is available to restore the database to its original factory state (Use with caution).

---

## 5. Summary of User Roles
*   **Viewer (Department Head)**:
    *   Log in.
    *   Filter data by their Function/SBU.
    *   View Skills Matrix and Proficiency expectations.
*   **Admin (HR)**:
    *   All Viewer capabilities.
    *   Switch to Table View.
    *   Edit Proficiency Levels.
    *   Add/Delete Records.
